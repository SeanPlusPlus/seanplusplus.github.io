title: "Code Review Is the Hello World of Agents"
date: 2026-04-10
---

I was chatting with another engineer about AI agents and he mentioned he was prototyping a code review bot. I've been running one in production for a while now and the more we talked, the more I realized: this is the project everyone should start with. It's the hello world of the agentic era — small enough to ship in a weekend, complex enough to teach you everything that matters: tool use, prompt engineering, failure modes, and the humbling gap between demo and production.
